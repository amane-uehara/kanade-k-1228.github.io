## ディラック方程式

$$
\left[i\hbar \gamma^\mu \pd{x^\mu}-mc\right] \psi(x) = 0

### ガンマ行列

$$
\gamma^0 = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1
\end{pmatrix}
\gamma^i = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & \sigma_i \\ -\sigma_i & 0
\end{pmatrix}
$$

#### 性質

$$
\begin{aligned}
\gamma^\mu\gamma^\nu+\gamma^\nu\gamma^\mu &= g^{\mu\nu} \\
(\gamma^\mu)^\dagger &= g_\mu^\mu\gamma^\mu \\
(\gamma^\mu)^2 &= g_\mu^\mu \\
\gamma^\mu\gamma^\nu &= -\gamma^\nu\gamma^\mu  
\end{aligned}
$$
